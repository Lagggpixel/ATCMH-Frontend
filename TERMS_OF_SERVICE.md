The Service helps ATCMH operate mentorship and attendance workflows. Depending on your role, it may:

- Display public attendance leaderboard information.
- Let staff view and manage sessions, mentees, user notes, assignment templates, manuals, analytics, and audit logs.
- Let users and staff run Discord slash commands for attendance, notifications, sessions, mentee status, user notes, recruiter and mentor records, tickets, and administrative actions.

## 3. Accounts, Authentication, and Linked Services

ATCMH operates central authentication with secure opaque sessions for the Dashboard and Exams Center. You may sign in through Discord or Infinite Flight Community. After a provider verifies your identity, ATCMH issues its own session; provider access tokens and identity claims are not placed in browser-readable login cookies or browser query parameters. Dashboard staff access depends on current Discord roles and may be removed when roles change.

The Service may link your Discord and Infinite Flight Community identities to one ATCMH account. If you sign in with an unlinked Infinite Flight Community identity, you may be required to verify a Discord identity before access is granted. ATCMH blocks conflicting links for review instead of silently transferring an identity between accounts. Authorised administrators may manage, correct, merge, suspend, or remove account links where operationally necessary, with those actions recorded in account history and audit records.

Before ATCMH issues a Dashboard session or Exams Center login handoff, you must accept the current required versions of these Terms and the Privacy Policy. Acceptance applies across both applications. If either document's required version changes, existing sessions may become invalid and you must accept the current documents before logging in again. ATCMH records the document versions, document hashes, account, accepting application, time, and login IP address as evidence of acceptance.

You are responsible for keeping your Discord and Infinite Flight Community accounts secure. Do not share browser sessions, staff access, or credentials with anyone else.
